Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is Chart AI's analysis?

Chart AI utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms trained on millions of chart patterns and market data points. While no analysis tool can guarantee 100% accuracy, our users report significantly improved trading decisions with success rates well above manual analysis.

What chart types can the app analyze?

Chart AI can analyze various financial chart types including candlestick charts, line charts, bar charts, and point & figure charts. Our AI is trained to recognize patterns across all major chart formats used in trading.

How do I cancel my premium subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime through the app's settings menu or through your app store account. Your premium access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Which languages are supported?

Chart AI currently supports 15 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Russian, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, Turkish, and Swedish. We regularly add new languages based on user demand.

Is my chart data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All chart uploads are encrypted, and you can opt to have your data deleted immediately after analysis. We never share your charts or analysis results with third parties.
