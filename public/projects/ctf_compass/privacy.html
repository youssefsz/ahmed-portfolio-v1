Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2024

CTF Compass ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices regarding the CTF Compass mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing or using the Service, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our Service.

1. No Personal Data Collection

CTF Compass is designed with privacy in mind. Our core philosophy is to provide functionality without collecting personal data:

  • We do not require account creation or user registration
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not request, store, or process payment information
  • We do not track individual user activity or create user profiles

This approach allows you to use our Service without concern about personal data being collected.

2. Limited Automatic Data Collection

While we do not collect personal information, our Service may automatically collect certain non-identifiable information for operational purposes:

  • App crash reports (to improve stability)
  • Anonymous usage statistics (to improve functionality)
  • Device type and operating system (for compatibility purposes)

This information is aggregated, anonymous, and cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that collect information to provide essential functionality:

3.1 Advertising Services

The free version of our app displays advertisements. Advertising providers may use technologies to display relevant ads. These providers may collect and use non-personal information about your visits to our app to provide better advertising experiences. For information about opting out of interest-based advertising, visit http://www.aboutads.info/choices/ or http://www.youronlinechoices.eu/.

3.2 Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools to help us measure traffic patterns and usage of the Service. These tools collect information anonymously and report app trends without identifying individual visitors.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has used our Service, please contact us at support@abidiahmed.com.

5. Security

Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk of data exposure. However, we still implement reasonable security measures to protect the integrity of our Service.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

7. Your Choices

As our Service doesn't collect personal information, there is no personal data to manage. However, you can:

  • Disable ad personalization on your device settings
  • Use device-level settings to limit information sharing

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@abidiahmed.com.
