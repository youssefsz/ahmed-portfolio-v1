Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the exchange rates?

CurrEx retrieves exchange rates from multiple reliable financial data providers and updates them every minute to ensure the highest accuracy possible.

Does CurrEx work offline?

Yes! CurrEx caches the most recent exchange rates when you're online, allowing you to perform conversions even without an internet connection.
